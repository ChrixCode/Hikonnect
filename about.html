About Hi-Konnect Network Limited

OUR STORY

Welcome to Hi-Konnect Network Limited, your gateway to a seamless online experience. We are more than just an Internet Service Provider; we are your partners in connectivity, dedicated to delivering the fastest and most reliable internet services to empower your digital world.

Vision

Our company, Hi-konnect Network Limited, aspires to be the most reputable provider of broadband internet and broadband services in Nigeria. To establish a full-spectrum telecoms institution of choice that stands out for its outstanding quality of service and constantly generates value for all stakeholders through cutting-edge goods that are supplied by high-caliber staff using the most cutting-edge technologies.

Mission

Our goal is to become Nigeria’s top Internet service provider, known for its outstanding customer service, professionalism, dependability, flexibility, responsiveness, and innovative offerings of goods and services. Our goal is a united Africa. Through the use of internet technology, our networks aid in the expansion of enterprises and the improvement of personal life.

Experience the future of connectivity with Hi-Konnect Network Limited. Discover the speed, reliability, and customer support that sets us apart. Connect with us today and elevate your online experience to new heights. Hi-Konnect Network Limited is one of the fastest growing broadband internet service providers in Nigeria designed and established to meet the wide range of internet needs of Nigerians by providing uninterrupted, Superfast, reliable and first-class internet services. Our primary objective at Hi-Konnect is to connect you to the Global World without any restriction and help you maximize every opportunity on the internet by serving you uncapped We are also relentlessly committed to you by providing better, faster, and more dependable broadband Internet at an unbeatable value. We offer several plans that fits all businesses and residential needs regardless of your data consumption. We are registered in Nigeria by the Corporate Affairs Commission with RC: 1981368. Our customer service team is available 24/7 to address and rectify any issue between 24-48 hours. We currently have our base station in Ikorodu, and our goal is to expand our threshold and meet the needs of other Nigerians in other Location.
